About

A Little Background

I'm Amed — a cybersecurity student with a deep curiosity for how things work under the hood. When I'm not breaking into systems (ethically), I'm probably making music, exploring Linux, or tinkering with hardware.

I believe in learning by doing. Most of what I know came from late nights in front of a terminal, rebuilding broken systems, and asking "what happens if I do this?" My interests span from penetration testing and reverse engineering to music production and photography.

This site is my personal space — part portfolio, part mixtape, part journal.
